SatyaSetu is an AI-powered mobile application designed to promote transparency and support Sustainable Development Goal 16 (Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions). The app enables users to anonymously report suspected corruption incidents by submitting details and supporting evidence. AI assists in organizing and analyzing reports, while a secure dashboard helps authorities review submissions efficiently. SatyaSetu aims to encourage accountability, ethical governance, and citizen participation.
